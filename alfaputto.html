projects about

Alfaputto

I and my group of friends were walking in Urbino and we were searching images to create a new typeface inspired by the city. We decided to go to Palazzo Ducale where there was a lot of medieval and renaissance picture. There we decided to collect a lot of figures of paintings subjects or details and used them to compose our alphabet. We decided to use black and white images to create a fusion with images and letters shapes. As you can see the result could be a modern version of medieval capital first letters depicted inside the old holy book by calligraphy monks.
