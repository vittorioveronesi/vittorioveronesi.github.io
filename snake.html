Snake-rope logo

The new logo for “Vds climbing 2.0” association. The new logo for the climbing association “Val di Sole climbing 2.0” is the fusion of a snake and a climbing rope. It is common in the field of “sports brands” to use animal figures to communicate complex human abilities. For example, some animal represents pride, others the bravery, and other again the strategy... In this case, the snake reveals the fear of falling (instinct part) or the fear of failure. Differently, the node represents the technical ability(reason part). The two symbols together are, in my opinion, the main aspects of sport climbing.
