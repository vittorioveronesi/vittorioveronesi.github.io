Vittorio Veronesi, Vermiglio 1996

I am a graphic designer with a Bachelor degree in graphic design and visual communication at the Isia Urbino.
I have worked like a carpenter in my dad’s workshop
for an undefined period (I am still hanging out in the workshop). I have learned the craftsmanship’s foundations which I have adopted in my graphic works.
My work investigates the visual communication of the new media. I am focus on the influence of the new digital devices on our education and our day routine.
