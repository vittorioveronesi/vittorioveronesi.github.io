My external hard drive is an artist.

I was in Urbino where I was studying for a university exam. Suddenly my external hard drive decided to crash and I lost all of my necessary exam files. I went to the nearest computer services to see if there was a possibility to get back my files. Luckily the computer technician gave me a new hard drive full of my holy exams after some hours of work and he said he retrieved my files but probably some of them were damaged. So, when I opened my folder, I found a lot of strange and original masterpieces that my hard drive decided to create. I found it amazing, like a sci-fi story where the chaos (if we can call it like that) becomes the decision-maker of amazing visual work. It is normal to be afraid of errors but sometimes they are a lesson for an easier life.

My hard drive loves to paint different kinds of plants or huge green landscapes sometimes with a cloudy sky, sometimes with a pink sunset. He also produced a portrait of Paul Verlaine.

I supposed this is a self-portrait...
