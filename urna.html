Urna type is an experimental typeface born in Bari during a Luciano Perondi and Alessio D’ellena’s workshop. I was drawing different letters in only 5 minutes: this was the workshop first process, “You must draw really quickly until you lost your common style and you start design without prejudices”. During this phase I noticed interesting shapes where the letter were built with wrong inktrap*. Sometimes errors became really useful for the creative process.

*The inktrap is a white space positioned around the letter’s corners. Its reason for being is to avoid the accumulation of ink.
