During my work experience at “Brave New Alps” studio I designed the structure and wrote the code of “Comun’orto” website. Comun’orto is a project born in Rovereto with the intention to promote commoning and community-building processes. The customer asked me a website concise and eco-friendly. I decided to follow the rules that I learned from the workshop with Joana Moll and built a website where you can find all the information but, at the same time, it doesn’t present unnecessary elements unhealthy for the planet.
For more information about how much co2 a website page emits click on the name of the artist.
