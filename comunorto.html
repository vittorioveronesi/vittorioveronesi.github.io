The Art of Asking Your Boss for a Raise

I was reading the book “The Art of Asking Your Boss for a Raise” by Georges Perec and I found it interesting. The author decided to not use punctuation in the entire book: the text is composed of one only sentence. I think about it and I would like to give a shape of this work that expresses the particular characteristic of this book. In conclusion, I took a thermal print and I used it to print a long sentence... the entire Perec's book.
